Our Story

Experience To Be Trusted

Hireandbuild is a one stop solution for all your construction related needs. If you are looking for professional construction services in chennai your search ends here. We provide finest quality construction services

Hireandbuild was started out of necessity after listening to the pain points of customer in finding a building contractor and managing the project without any quality issues, time and cost over-run. We decided to be a change, we wanted to see in the residential construction industry.

“It All Starts With Why”

-Simon Sinek

Pain Point of a Customer

  • Finding a residential building contractors in Chennai is not the only problem...
  • Some contractors use poor quality materials, delay projects, and provide shabby work.
  • Many people want to build a dream home but are unable to because of this difficulty.
  • The world is evolving towards online solutions but not for construction.
  • We started researching and found very few places giving transparent details of construction service providers.

"We kept on listening to the customer's pain points"

Finally, we came up with the idea of creating hireandbuild. A company that provides end to end construction services according to the customer needs & budget.

We provide the best quality of work and the best value to the customer. We send regular updates to the customer through mobile app, maintain all the records, designs & financials in the app to be available all the time.

A complete transparent process to ensure the work is of the maximum quality.

“Quality Is Not A Process; It's A Habit”
